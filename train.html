What is Live Train Running Status?

Live train running status for Indian railway trains means the current location of any train and its real time delay status. It also includes estimated arrival time of the train at upcoming stops.

Why do users need to check current train running status frequently on the date of the journey?

It is critical to save on time while waiting to board your train or booking a taxi when you reach your destination. Train running status updates every minute. So, we recommend that our users check the status updates from time to time. The train running status information becomes more and more accurate, as the train is about to reach the estimated time of travel. This information helps you save time and plan better!

How can we check Live Train Status?

Train status can be checked on the go via website to know the current location of the train and its delay status. Check train status through any modern day Internet-powered devices. If you are on a train you can also check live train status being offline by switching to GPS mode for fetching live train status. It recommends its users to use GPS mode while travelling on train for faster and more precise location.

What is PNR?

Full form of PNR is Passenger Name Record, a unique 10-digit number which is provided against every train ticket that is booked across India. Each PNR numbers provides a variety of information to ticket-holder(s).

The PNR Status information includes ticket booking status (confirmed/waiting list/canceled), departure and arrival time, details of the train, seat number, fare paid by the passenger for the seat. When group booking is done, then a single PNR may be provided to up to 6 passengers.

How to check IRCTC PNR status online?

To check PNR status, follow these steps:

1. Find 10-digit PNR number on top left of your IRCTC train ticket.
2. Enter the PNR No. of your ticket and click "Get Status".
3. Your PNR Status will show up with information regarding your train ticket booking.
